The James Cancer Hospital at The Ohio State University

The James is the third-largest cancer hospital in the nation and the only NCI designated hospital in the midwest. They have one of the finest reputations for patient care and research. With more than 300 cancer researchers and 200 specialized cancer physicians, The James is changing the way cancer is detected, treated and cured.

THE CHALLENGE

How do you re-imagine a broken website and digital ecosystem for a world-renowned cancer hospital that is trying to serve seven unique audiences?

OUR BRUTAL SIMPLICITY OF THOUGHT

We needed to refocus on the patient.

  • Recreate the website experience, design and content
  • Foster a community by investing in social channels and promoting user generated content
  • Increase traffic by placing ourselves in our patients’ path
  • Create an ecosystem that feeds itself

THE RESULTS

A digital ecosystem worthy of a world-renowned hospital.

THE WORK

Website

We developed a new website framed around the patient’s experience. It was a huge undertaking: 7 separate pieces of research guided the development of 17 unique wireframe templates and designs, a custom SEO strategy was developed to optimize the website for search, 1,700 pages of content were created, 7 backend systems were integrated and all content was migrated to a Sitecore CMS system.

VISIT THE SITE

BLOG

The James Blog plays a vital role by dynamically communicating the hospital's mission to patients as well as healthcare professionals. As a leader in cancer care and research, the blog created a platform that allowed the hospital to be a source for patient-friendly cancer updates and ground-breaking research news. The James Blog has become an integral part of the online ecosystem by driving significant traffic to the website and social channels.

SOCIAL

Social media is a vital part of building a relationship with patients and fostering a community amongst patients and their families. We translated The James’ strategy and positioning to their social pagesso they became part of the brand. We then created a content strategy, reporting structure and promotional plan that helped increase their presence across the social space and tripled their levels of engagement.

MAMM-O-GRAM APP

We developed a Mamm-o-Gram tablet application that allowed women to record video reminders to book a mammogram. The video could then be emailed to a friend or loved one, shared to the user’s social channels or sent to themselves 5 months later as a personal reminder. The app created buzz and user generated content that helped increase mammograms and clinical volume at The James by 20%.

PAID CONTENT DISCOVERY

To drive our target audience to The James Blog, we ran a paid discovery campaign that pushed our content on select sites including MSN, NBC Fox, CNN and ABC. The campaign increased daily visits to the blog by 984% and increased unique visitors by 1,149%. As a result we saw an overall lift in traffic to our online properties, such as the website.
