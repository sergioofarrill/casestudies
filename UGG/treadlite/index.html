THE TASK

To launch UGG Australia's new line of lightweight men's shoes that feature an innovative, outsole technology called Treadlite.

Treadlite delivers the iconic comfort of UGG® with the added benefits of extreme lightness, flexibility, durability and grip on both wet and dry surfaces.

BRUTAL SIMPLICTY OF THOUGHT

By combining the lightness, flexibility, and grip of Treadlite with a stylish, shoe-like upper, men, now, have lighter way to go about their daily commute.

THE IDEA

If UGG® truly makes shoes that feel like nothing else, then what does lightness feel like?

DIGITAL

Page take-overs, progressive and static banners

SOCIAL

Tease silhouette images were released in Paris, London, Shanghai, Tokyo, Amsterdam & NYC.

Social media postings and other marketing elements highlighted the ultra light experience offered by Treadlite, while also showcasing the fashion, flexibility and grip Treadlite provides.

RESULTS

NEAR SELL-OUT!

Major online retail partners saw >90% sell through in the first week. Treadlite Freamon has been the #1 selling men's style from the Spring 2015 collection.
