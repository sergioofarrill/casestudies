How We Got Men to Wear a Woman's Shoe

How did you tell a story that people don't want to hear?

How did we convince men to wear - and love - a women's brand?

And how did we enable UGG® For Men to achieve near sellout on a new style, making this their best-selling non-slipper product ever?

THE TASK

UGG® For Men had a new product story. Their Treadlite TM sole had been designed to deliver the same iconic comfort the brand is famous for, but with the added benefits of lightness, flexibility, durability and grip.

Our job was to make this story relevant - and desirable - to today's man.

BRUTAL SIMPLICTY OF THOUGHT

By combining the lightness, flexibility, and grip of Treadlite with a stylish, shoe-like upper, men, now, have lighter way to go about their daily commute.

THE IDEA

Treadlite TM by UGG®.
A lighter way to go.

THE WORK

Page take-overs, progressive and static banners

Tease silhouette images were released in Paris, London, Shanghai, Tokyo, Amsterdam & NYC.

Social media, Ecomm and in-store assets showcased the ultra-light experience offered by Treadlite TM,
whilst also highlighting the other product benefits such as durability and grip.

AN EVOLUTION

The spring launch was so successful that Treadlite TM was rolled out to a number of other styles, both Men's and Women's.

For fall, and sturdy weather boots, this meant a new challenge. How did we communicate both lightness and toughness? And how did we position lightness as a benefit for a Men's weather-proof shoe?

RESULTS

In the first week of the spring campaign online retail partners experienced >90% sell-through.

Zappos sold out completely.

The Freamon is still the #1 selling Men's non-slipper style in UGG For Men history.
